skills in the subject</p>
<p><strong>B</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Has good knowledge and
skills in the subject</p>
<p><strong>C</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Has adequate knowledge and
skills in the subject</p>
