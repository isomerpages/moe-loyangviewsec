<h1>Parent Support Group @ LVSS</h1>
<p><strong><em>Supporting our children, together as a community</em></strong>
</p>
<p>
<br><strong>Why join PSG?</strong>
</p>
<p>The Parent Support Group (PSG) is a great opportunity for us to be closer
to our children, understand them better, and help them in their education
journey. Our children also benefit when we are more active in their school
environment.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PSG/PSG1.jpg">
</div>
<p>They tend to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>show more positive attitude</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>have better school attendance and complete homework more consistently</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>be able to bridge the cultural gap between school and home</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>be less prone to behavioural issues</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;
<br><strong>Who are we?</strong>
</p>
<p>At LVSS PSG, we are a community of parents who come together to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>journey with our children during school events</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>organise activities to strengthen parent-child bonding</p>
</li>
</ul>

<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PSG/PSG4.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>lend support for a meaningful cause</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
