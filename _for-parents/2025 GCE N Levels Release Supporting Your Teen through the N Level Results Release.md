<p>Dear Parents/Guardians,&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>The 2025 N-Level results will be released soon. While we await the results,
let us also remember to celebrate their efforts, regardless of the results.
We can show support for our teens by showing them through our words and
actions that their grades do not define who they are.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Your support to your teen during this milestone is important. Use this
opportunity to have open conversations and guide them as they consider
their next steps.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Collection of GCE N-Level Results</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All students are to return to their Secondary schools to collect their
results. Students are to be seated in the School Hall by 1.45 pm, in proper
school attire, including appropriate grooming. The results release will
commence at 2.00 pm. Those who are unable to collect their results in person
can appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results from the
school. Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents for the
