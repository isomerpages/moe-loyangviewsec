<br><strong>Admission Criteria</strong> 
<br>
<br>We offer DSA in two of our signature programmes:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;ALP – Design &amp; Engineering (<strong>S</strong>cience&nbsp;<strong>T</strong>echnology&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>ngineering&nbsp;<strong>M</strong>aths)</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;LLP – Sports &amp; Outdoor Education
(Character Through Sports Exposure, Sports Empowerment &amp; Sports Excellence)</p>
<p>Find out more about&nbsp;<a href="https://www.loyangviewsec.moe.edu.sg/programmes/Applied-Learning-Programme-ALP/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">ALP</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;
<a href="https://www.loyangviewsec.moe.edu.sg/programmes/Learning-For-Life-Programme-LLP/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">LLP</a>.</p>
<p>ALP –Design &amp; Engineering (STEM)</p>
<p>Applicants must be able to demonstrate an aptitude and/or interest in
STEM through any of the following:</p>
<p>(a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in STEM-related competitions
at school, cluster or national level.</p>
<p>(b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in and showcase of STEM-related projects
at school, cluster or national level.</p>
<p>(c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in STEM-related workshops, enrichment
programmes, symposiums, etc.</p>
<p>(d)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in STEM-related Learning Journeys (local
or overseas).</p>
<p>(e)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sustained participation in Science or Maths related
CCA.</p>
<p>(f)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Self-initiated participation in Science
or Maths-related activities outside of school.</p>
<p></p>
<p>LLP – Sports &amp; Outdoor Education</p>
<p>Applicants who have the talent or potential in the following sports are
welcome to apply.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shooting (Boys
&amp; Girls)
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basketball (Boys
only)
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Floorball (Boys
only
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Netball (Girls
only)
<br>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rock-Climbing (Boys
&amp; Girls)</p>
<p><strong>Applicants must also meet the following criteria.</strong>
</p>
<p>(a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in local/international sports
competition or event as a representative of the primary school, or</p>
<p>(b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participation in local/international sports-related
competition or event as a representative for an external club (if current
primary school does not offer the sport as a CCA)</p>
<p><strong><em>Meeting all the criteria does not guarantee the student will be shortlisted/given an offer.</em></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Application Process (How to apply)</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Centralised DSA-Sec Application</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal.
The application is free-of-charge and will be open from&nbsp;<strong>7 May 2025 to 3 June 2025</strong>.
For more details, please refer to MOE website –&nbsp;<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>.</p>
<p>Our school will shortlist applicants and conduct in-person interview and
trial with shortlisted applicants from&nbsp;<strong>1 July 2025 to 28 August 2025.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Outcome of Application</strong>
</p>
<p>All applicants will be informed of the results of their DSA-Sec application
through a letter by&nbsp;between&nbsp;<strong>25 August and 4 September 2025.</strong>
</p>
<p>Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are expected
to honour their commitment to the school, and participate in the activities
related to the talent they are selected for from year 1-4.&nbsp;Loyang
View Secondary School invites all current Primary 6 students to apply for
admission to our school in the academic year of 2026.</p>
<p>Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on
the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic
and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop
the area of talent.</p>
