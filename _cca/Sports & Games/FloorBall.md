<h1>FloorBall</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>LV Floorball wants to develop future-ready individuals who are not only
skilled athletes but also lifelong learners and active citizens. We aim
to foster a love for physical fitness and teamwork while instilling important
values such as discipline, resilience, and leadership. Through sports,
our members are encouraged to embrace continuous learning, adapt to challenges,
and contribute positively to their communities, empowering them to lead
healthy, impactful lives both now and in the future.</p>
