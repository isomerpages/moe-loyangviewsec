<h2>Our Creed</h2>
<h5><strong><em>I am unique and worthy.</em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>Within me lies the power to be the best that I can be. </em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>I look forward to each day with a sense of purpose; </em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>To be a wholesome individual with robust character; </em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>Willing to work hard, learn, and care for others.</em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>My future can be what I want it to be. </em></strong></h5>
<h5><strong><em>With resilience I will succeed.</em></strong></h5>
